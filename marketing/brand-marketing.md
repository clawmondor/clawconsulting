# Brand & Marketing Strategy
## Claw Consulting - AI Consulting for Local Businesses

## 1. Brand Identity

### Name Options

| Option | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| **Claw Consulting** | Bold, memorable, stands out from generic "AI Solutions" names. Implies grabbing opportunities. Works well with lobster/robot branding. |
| **Claw AI** | Cleaner, more focused on AI specifically. Easier to build as a standalone brand. Short and punchy. |
| **LocalIQ Consulting** | Descriptive - signals AI expertise + local focus. More traditional consulting feel, easier to explain in ads. |

**Recommendation:** Go with **Claw Consulting** - it's distinctive, memorable, and gives you room to expand into other services beyond just AI.

### Tagline Options

- "AI that actually works for your business."
- "Smart tech. Local know-how."
- "Cutting-edge AI. No enterprise pricing."
- "Your competitive edge, powered by AI."

**Recommended:** "AI that actually works for your business." - Clear, promises value, sets expectations.

### Visual Direction

- **Primary color:** Coral orange (#FF5A36) - energy, action, stands out
- **Secondary:** Dark (#0F0F0F) - modern, sleek background
- **Font:** DM Sans - clean, modern, accessible
- **Iconography:** Lobster claw motif 🦞 - distinctive, memorable
- **Vibe:** Professional but not corporate. Think "helpful neighbor who's really good with tech" rather than "Silicon Valley startup."

## 5. Advertising Channel Recommendations

| Channel | Est. Monthly Cost | Why | Best For |
|---------|-------------------|-----|----------|
| **Google Local Services Ads** | $300-800/mo | Shows at top of search when people look for "AI consultant near me" | High intent leads |
| **NextDoor** | $200-500/mo | Hyper-local targeting, local business owners | Neighborhood businesses |
| **Facebook/Instagram** | $300-600/mo | Broad targeting, visual ads work well | Awareness, retargeting |
| **LinkedIn** | $400-800/mo | B2B local consultants, professional services | Higher-ticket services |
| **Yelp Ads** | $150-400/mo | Local business discovery | Retail, restaurants, salons |

**Recommended Budget:** $800-1,500/month start
- 50% Google Local Services (high intent)
- 30% Facebook/Instagram (awareness + retargeting)
- 20% NextDoor (local reach)
